“Things I wish I had known about R spatial” & Problem solving session IV

To end the year with a bang, we’re doing a double session this month.

We will start with a short session on Spatial Analysis in R - Things I wish I had known about R spatial by Christina Buelow.

For the second half we will have another problem solving session. As with previous problem-solving sessions, we will have a free-range discussion with the goal of helping people get back on track with their geospatial data analysis. If you have a thorny problem, feel free to send it through to Nicholas Wiggins (n.wiggins@uq.edu.au) ahead of the session and we will try to come up with a solution.



When Thursday 24th November, 2:30 - 4:30pm

Where Online, a teams link will be sent out via email
